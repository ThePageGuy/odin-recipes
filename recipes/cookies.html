Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Homemade chocolate chip cookies with a perfect balance of soft and chewy, and loaded with delicious chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients, followed by the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
