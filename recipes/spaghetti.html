Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, rich, and incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and mix in the grated cheese and black pepper.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and immediately toss with the egg mixture and pancetta.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with extra cheese and black pepper.
